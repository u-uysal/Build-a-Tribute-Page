Mustafa Kemal ATATURK

The man who the founder of the Republic of Turkey

Here's a time line of Mustafa Kemal ATATURK's life:

  • 1881 - Mustafa Kemal Atatürk was born in Salonika (now Thessaloniki) in what was then the Ottoman Empire.
  • 1905 - He was sent to military school and then to the military academy in Istanbul and graduating .
  • 1911 - He served against the Italians in Libya and then in the Balkan Wars (1912 - 1913).
  • 1915 - He made his military reputation repelling the Allied invasion at the Dardanelles.
  • 1919 - Atatürk began a nationalist revolution in Anatolia, organising resistance to the peace settlement imposed on Turkey by the victorious Allies.
  • 1921 - Atatürk established a provisional government in Ankara.
  • 1923 - Turkey became a secular republic with Atatürk as its president.
  • 1935 - When surnames were introduced in Turkey, he was given the name Atatürk, meaning 'Father of the Turks'.
  • 1938 - He died on 10 November 1938.
